01 AT A GLANCE

The founder-controlled boutique

Gestifute is the player-representation agency founded by Jorge Mendes in 1996, headquartered in Porto. The agency operates on a founder-controlled boutique model with a roster of approximately 150-200 players, materially smaller than CAA Stellar or Wasserman by headcount but with a higher concentration of top-tier and consequential clients.

The agency's representation of Cristiano Ronaldo since the early-2000s has been the platform's defining commercial relationship. The Mourinho relationship, the dense Iberian and South American roster, and the embedded relationships with Portuguese clubs (Porto, Benfica, Sporting) and Spanish clubs constitute the agency's structural advantage.

03 OPERATING REALITY

What the institution actually does

Gestifute's operating model is the structural opposite of CAA Stellar's. The agency runs on founder-led concentration: Jorge Mendes is personally involved in most major client engagements, the relationships with Portuguese, Spanish, and Brazilian clubs are personal-relationship-led, and the agency has not pursued the broader-platform multi-discipline model that the institutional-PE-backed agencies have built.

The Cristiano Ronaldo representation since the early 2000s remains the platform's defining commercial relationship. The 2022 transfer to Al-Nassr — and the broader Saudi Pro League marquee-signings wave that followed — was substantially shaped by Mendes's relationships with Saudi sporting decision-makers. The agency's role in the Saudi football ecosystem during 2022-2024 was disproportionate to its global headcount.

The succession question is the structural overhang. Mendes founded Gestifute and runs it personally; no public succession architecture has been articulated. The agency's institutional value depends substantially on Mendes's continued direct involvement.

04 STRATEGIC POSTURE

Direction in 2026

Gestifute's stated direction in 2026 is founder-led continuity. The agency continues to represent its core roster, manage existing client relationships, and selectively add new high-profile representations. No public expansion programme into women's football, executive search, or other adjacent practices has been articulated.

The Saudi Pro League relationship continues to be a meaningful component of the agency's recent transaction flow, but the post-2024 cooling of the marquee-signings era reduces the volume of Mendes-led Saudi transactions relative to the 2022-2024 peak.
