At a glance

The venue-operations platform

ASM Global is the leading venue management and operations platform globally, providing operational management, F&B, event programming, and broader venue-services across 350+ stadiums, arenas, convention centres, and theatres globally. The football-specific footprint is selective — major European football clubs typically run venues directly — but ASM Global has presence across selected stadium operations and broader sport-event-hosting venues.

The platform is co-owned by AEG (entertainment-and-sport conglomerate, anchored by the Anschutz Corporation) and Onex Partners (PE), formed via the 2019 merger of AEG Facilities and SMG.

Operating reality

What the institution actually does

ASM Global operates a long-term venue-management contracting model. Venue counterparties (city authorities, federation bodies, private venue owners) contract ASM Global to operate venues across operations, F&B, event-programming, and broader services. The platform's scale across 350+ venues drives operating efficiencies and event-routing benefits that smaller competitors cannot match.

The football-specific footprint is structurally smaller than the broader portfolio — top-flight European clubs typically operate venues directly via club-owned operations subsidiaries. ASM Global's football-relevant positioning is on multi-use stadiums, broader sport-event venues, and selected European secondary-tier positions.

Strategic posture

Direction in 2026

ASM Global's stated direction in 2026 is integrated venue management at scale. Continued global expansion remains a stated growth thread. Sport-and-entertainment venue compounding continues. Selective acquisitive posture against smaller venue-management competitors continues.
