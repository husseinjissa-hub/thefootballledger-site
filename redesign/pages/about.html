Our principles

How we
stay neutral.

Analysis, not description

We quantify, model, and connect the dots — we don't narrate the news.

Forward-looking implications

We focus on what comes next, not just what already happened.

The contrarian read

We challenge consensus when the evidence points in a different direction.

Specific over general

Named entities, numbers, transactions, and dates — because precision drives credibility.
