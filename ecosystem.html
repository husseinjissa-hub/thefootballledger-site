The Ecosystem at a Glance
01
Governing bodies
The regulators and federations that set the rules of competition, governance, and national-team football.

Traditional leagues & competitions
The leagues and competitions where the game is played, fans are built, and rights are won.

Sportainment leagues
A parallel league tier built around non-traditional competition formats — typically 6- or 7-a-side.

Clubs & multi-club ownership
The clubs at the center of the system and the ownership groups building portfolios.

Capital — sovereign, PE, family, debt

Agencies & representation
Agents and agencies representing players, managers, and clubs in the global market.

06
Media & broadcasting
Broadcasters and streaming platforms distributing football to billions of fans.
Apple Canal+ Sky Group SSC and Shahid
07
Commercial — kit, sponsor, retail
Brands and platforms powering commercial revenues and fan engagement.
EA Sports Etihad Airways Fanatics New Balance Riyadh Air / AlUla / NEOM Two Circles

Football-tech, data & performance
Technology and data companies driving performance, operations, and insights.
Jamestown Analytics SkillCorner Sportradar TransferRoom

Stadium, matchday & fan experience
Stadium operators, venues, and platforms enhancing the fan and matchday experience.
