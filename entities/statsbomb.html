At a glance

The football-data analytics anchor

StatsBomb is the leading football-specific data analytics platform globally, with anchor positions across European top-flight clubs (selected Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1), federations (selected national associations), and the broader recruitment, opposition-analysis, and academy-development markets. The platform's proprietary event-data, tracking-data, and analytic-modelling architecture is the structural differentiator versus traditional sports-data competitors.

Stats Perform reportedly acquired StatsBomb in 2024, integrating the platform into the broader Stats Perform group while continuing the StatsBomb brand identity and product line.

Operating reality

What the institution actually does

StatsBomb's operating model is subscription data plus analytic-platform access. Clubs subscribe to event-data, tracking-data, and the StatsBomb IQ recruitment platform, with custom-modelling consultancy layered for top-end clients. The proprietary tracking-data architecture (StatsBomb 360) is the structural differentiator versus competitors that depend on third-party tracking feeds.

The Stats Perform integration consolidates the broader football-data category. The combined entity has the deepest event-and-tracking data footprint globally, with overlapping client bases that drive cross-sell and account-deepening opportunities.

Strategic posture

Direction in 2026

StatsBomb's stated direction in 2026 is data-platform deepening across club workflows: continued recruitment-platform expansion, opposition-analysis tooling, and academy-development integration. The Stats Perform integration architecture continues. Federation-side expansion remains a stated growth thread.
