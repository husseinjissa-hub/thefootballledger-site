Join the Ledger.

We're looking for people who understand that football is a business.

Who we're looking for

The Ledger is built by volunteers who want a foothold in the business of football. You don't need a media background — just curiosity, rigour, and a point of view. Contributors come from a wide range of places:

Business & financial analysts

Consulting, finance, or other professional backgrounds — people who can turn a deal, a filing, or a capital move into a sharp, sourced argument.

University students

Students in business, economics, law, or sports management looking for real analytical reps and a byline that shows how they think.

Regional correspondents

Eyes on specific markets — MENA, the USA, LatAm, Asia — where the next cycle of capital is moving.

Freelancers

One-off deep-dives welcome. If you have a structural argument and the evidence for it, send it.

Where you'd help

Ledger support

Researching and building articles across the nine layers of the ecosystem — from governance and ownership to media rights and matchday.

Social media

Turning published analysis into a clear, credible presence on X, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

How we work

The Ledger is analysis, not reporting: neutral, third-person, evidence-led. Every piece is a structural argument with named entities, specific numbers, and a forward-looking implication. If that is how you already think about the game, you will fit here.

How to apply

Send a note and a sample of your writing, and tell us which lane fits you best.
